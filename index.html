TimeBomb by Eastroad

Biography

Eastroad are a four piece rock band from South London. They have played many live shows all over the country including the major UK music festival Glastonbury. Last year Eastroad sold out London's famous venue The Borderline (capacity 350) and continue to put on live shows that are hugely energetic. The band have recently been recording new material and are now back on the gigging circuit. They have had Local, national and international radio play with the last single Ain't Like The Movies and hope to build on that success with the next Album.
